ABOUT US

Its' more fun in the Philippines

Who are we

Tara Liwaliw Travel is an Adventure Tour Operator in the Philippines with more than 11 years of experience, dedicated to bringing you innovative and active travel experiences. We offer a trip for every adventurous spirit— with high quality standards, customized, with a strong focus on sustainable travel. We support local communities, environments and wildlife. We truly live and breathe sustainable living that's why all our trips include immersive experiences with locals which in turn supports the local economy, livelihood of people, crafts and the community. Tailor-Made Itineraries, you tell us your ideas about where you want to go and how you like to travel and we’ll simply piece it together using our expert knowledge and working with you every step of the way.

Vision Statement

It is our vision that in 2023 we want to be a leading example of a tour operator demonstrating sustainable tourism in Cusco. We want to grow by offering more and a wider range of unique tours, develop from travel agency to tour operator and expand our visibility on the web.

Mission Statement

Provide a unique travel experience for everybody in a sustainable, honest and transparent way.
